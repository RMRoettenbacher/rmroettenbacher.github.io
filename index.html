I am a Yale Center for Astronomy and Astrophysics postdoctoral fellow at Yale University where I work on stellar activity.

In particular, I image the surfaces of spotted stars with a variety of techniques, including long-baseline interferometry.

Before Yale, I was a postdoctoral fellow at Stockholm Univeristy, and I received my PhD from the University of Michigan.
